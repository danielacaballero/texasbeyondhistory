Diagram by Floyd Studer of ongoing work at Alibates 28 in July, 1932. The apparent excavators are identified beside each trench, including “FVS” (Floyd V. Studer), “Carolyn” (Studer), “Charles” (Renfroe), and “J. Robin” (Allen). During this period, Studer was assisted by various friends and members of the Panhandle-Plains Historical Society. Map from the PPHS archives; identifications by Chris Lintz.
