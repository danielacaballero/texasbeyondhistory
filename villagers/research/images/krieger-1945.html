Krieger visits Coetas Creek 55 while on a brief fact-finding trip to the Texas Panhandle in July, 1945. From left to right: Floyd Studer, Alex Krieger, Clarence Webb, and Clarence Webb, Jr. Studer obviously led the field tour. Clarence Webb, a medical doctor from Shreveport, Louisiana, was a first-class amateur archeologist and one of Krieger's collaborators. TARL archives.
