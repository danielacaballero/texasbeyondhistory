Room 1 at the Footprint site contained the multilated remains of as many as 32 individuals, presumably Antelope Creek villagers killed in an attack. The house was then burned and sometime later 10 adult skulls, presumably war trophies perhaps taken in revenge, were placed into a pit (shown in blue) dug into the rubble of the house. Adapted from Green 1986, courtesy Panhandle Archeology Society.
