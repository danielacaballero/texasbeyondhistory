Room 3 at the Footprint site in Potter County, Texas, had the unusual combination of picket-wall construction with exterior slabs and irregular rocks set in mud mortar. Excavated in 1964 by F. Earl Green prior to the filling of Sanford Reservoir (now Lake Meredith), the construction of this house was recognized as "aberrant" in comparison to the many other Antelope Creek structures known from the area.
