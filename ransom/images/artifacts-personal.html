Ransom and Sarah Williams Farmstead

Personal Items

 

Personal artifacts account for a relatively small portion of the total artifact assemblage, but these items are an important and informative part of the Williams farmstead material culture. These objects can reveal much about the individuals who lived at the site and provide insights into their health and hygiene. This functional group contains objects related to grooming, health, and medicine, along with alcohol and tobacco products. Enlarge the artifact images to learn more.
