Two views of Rock Wall D1.

These images show the widest section of rock wall observed in the corral complex. The top photo (a) is looking west along the top of Rock Wall D1, with a 1-m scale (39 inches) lying on top of the wall. Rock Wall E1 runs perpendicular in the background. The bottom photo (b) is looking northwest at Rock Wall D1. The cross-section shows a section of Rock Wall D1 that was about 6 ft wide.
