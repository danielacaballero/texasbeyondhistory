Ransom and Sarah Williams Farmstead

Animal Bones and Plant Remains

 

Animal bones and plant remains are important archeological evidence, especially when recovered from a historic farmstead. These remains reveal clues about the various domestic animals and plants raised on the farm, wild plants that were gathered, and wild animals that were hunted. The evidence is also important for understanding the family’s diet and what types of wood they were burning as fuel. Enlarge the images to learn more.
