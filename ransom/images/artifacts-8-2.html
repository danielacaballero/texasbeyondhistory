These specimens represent the diverse range of glass bottle types recovered from the Williams farmstead. The bottles shown here are as follows: (a) is a wide-mouth, 1/2-ounce, cobalt blue medicine bottle; (b), (c), and (d) are panel bottles for patent medicines in 1/2-ounce, 1-ounce, and 3.5-ounce sizes; (e) is a 1.5-ounce ink bottle with a screw-top finish; (f) is a 4-ounce liquor bottle of the shoo-fly type with a Brandy-finish neck and mouth; and (g) is a 6-ounce amber snuff bottle.
