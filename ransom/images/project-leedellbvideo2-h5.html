LeeDell Bunton, Sr., worked with members of the RSWFP team and helped to make the oral history project and public outreach efforts a success. In this video clip, Mr. Bunton talks about the connection between the archaeology of the Williams farmstead and the history of Antioch Colony, of which he is a descendant of its founding settlers.
