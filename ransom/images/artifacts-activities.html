Ransom and Sarah Williams Farmstead

Activities

 

This broad category contains 13 different functional subgroups representing a wide range of tasks that occurred on the Williams farm. As such, these artifacts are highly informative, revealing much about how the family interacted with the world around them. Among the 954 artifacts are items related to music, toys, writing, sewing, transportation, tools and machinery, horse tack, hunting and fishing, water storage, and collectibles.
