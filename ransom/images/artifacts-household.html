Ransom and Sarah Williams Farmstead

Household and Kitchen Artifacts

 

This category constitutes 65 percent of the identifiable artifacts from the site, and represents activities that typically occurred in the kitchen or in a domestic setting. This includes a variety of artifacts, from food storage jars, serving dishes, and cutlery, to lamp parts and home furnishings. All glass bottles, even those containing non-food items, such as liquor or medicine bottles, are discussed in this section. The abundance of glass fragments indicates that the Williams family consumed a fair number of commercial products that came packaged in manufactured glass containers.
