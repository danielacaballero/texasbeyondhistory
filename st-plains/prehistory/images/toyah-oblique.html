Cluster of animal bones representing a disposal feature at the Hinojosa site. While a large bison leg bone is prominent, this small concentration included the bones of six species from diverse habitats – bison, javelina, turtle, three different kinds of rodents, and deer or antelope. Most of the bones were broken while fresh and/or have cut marks – obvious signs of butchering – and some of the bones are burned.
