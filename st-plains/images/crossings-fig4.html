Arrow points from the Pampopa-Talon Crossing site indicate use of the site by native groups during the Late Prehistoric as well as early Historic Period, the latter indicated by the presence of Cuney and Guerrero points. A-B, Scallorn; C-D, Perdiz; E,O, Cuney; F, Guerrero; H-I, Perdiz-like, G, unidentified.
