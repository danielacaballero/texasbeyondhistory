Native ranch families used both elegant and utilitarian tableware, as indicated by pottery sherds found at the site. Shown are oriental porcelain (top), Mexican majolica (San Elizario blue on white as well as polychrome varieties), and glazed wares (bottom row). Quantities of bone-tempered unglazed Goliad ware made by native potters also were found at the ranch. CAR-UTSA photo.
