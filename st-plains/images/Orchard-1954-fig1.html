Paleoindian laneolate projectile points collected by C. D. Orchard in the vicinity of the Olmos Dam site. All of these specimens appear to be Late Paleoindian styles including Plainview, St Mary’s Hall, and Angostura. Early Paleoindian Clovis and Folsom points have also been reported from the vicinity as well as a wide assortment of later Archaic dart points. Orchard and Campbell 1954.
