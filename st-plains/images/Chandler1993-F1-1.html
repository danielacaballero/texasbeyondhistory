Two rusted iron projectile points (front and back faces of each shown) which were cut out with chisels. The specimen at top has seven tiny notches on the stem. It was found on the surface of 41ME74, a multi-component campsite on Hondo Creek. The other point was found on the surface in southwestern Medina County.
