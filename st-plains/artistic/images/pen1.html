Examples of ground stone ornaments from Starr and Zapata Counties, Texas, and northern Mexico. A, Rio Alamo near Mier, Tamaulipas; b, Salado River, Tamaulipas, Mexico; C, near El Sauz, Starr County, Texas; D, Falcon Lake shoreline, Zapata County, Texas; E, Nuevo Leon, Mexico, southwest of Reynosa. Drawings by Richard McReynolds (Chandler and Kumpe 1994: Figure 1).
