Cool evening breezes are easier to enjoy outdoors. The colorful and durable "cubre de cama" (bed cover or quilt) is made from polyester. One man insists that all the polyester pantsuits from the 1960s and 1970s found their way to northern Mexico where they provide bright, almost indestructible quilting material. Jaboncillos, Coahuila, Mexico. Photo by JoAnn Pospisil.
