E. Mott Davis examines Bone Bed 2 near rear of shelter at south end. The shaded excavation wall towards which he looks is along N30. The exposed bones to his left at head level are part of Bone Bed 3 where the burning stops short of the shelter wall. The Fiber Layer is visible above Bone Bed 3 as a dark lense in the excavation wall. Photo taken by Jonathan Davis in December, 1963.
