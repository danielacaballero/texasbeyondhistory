This contemporary drawing entitled "The Road to Indian Market is Filled with Potholes” by Delores Purdy Corcoran (2012), is part of a new TBH lesson plan that examines how ledger art was, and still is, an important medium of expression for Native Americans. The lesson, "Drawing our Lives: Plains Ledger Art Revisited," is correlated to the Schild Ledger Book. Written by TBH Educational Advisor Carol Schlenk and oriented to 7th-grade students, the lesson contatins a variety of primary documents to aid students in understanding Plains Indian history.
