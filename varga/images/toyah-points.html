Arrow points of the Toyah occupation. Among the more than 216 points recovered, type Perdiz—considered the hallmark of this late interval—predominates, but other types such as Cliffton, Scallorn, Guerrero, Edwards, Cuney, Bonham, and Harrell also were present. Of special  interest are the Padre and Harrell types, typically found in far distant regions. Cliffton points, previously thought to be preforms, were found to have microscopic indications of haft wear.
