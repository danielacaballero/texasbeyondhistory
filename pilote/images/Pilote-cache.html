The nine bifaces that make up the cache recovered from Cueva Pilote include one early stage perform (a), five middle stage preforms (b-f), one late stage perform (g), one finished Langtry dart point (h), and one finished Jora dart point (i). This cache predates the rest of the material from the cave by over 2000 years and may represent an earlier occupation of the site, or may have been found elsewhere and brought to the site by the latter people who used the cave for ritual activity.
