Work log and payroll from Glen Evans' notes lists the small start-up crew for the TMM project (including an individual named Woodrow Wilson, presumably not related to the former U.S. president). Paleontologist Grayson E. Meade was among those added to the crew, and anthropologists Thomas N. Campbell, Alex Krieger, and J. Charles Kelley were frequent visitors to the site during TMM excavations. TARL archives.
