Angostura points. This style of lanceolate dart tip was made during very Early Archaic times some 9000 years ago. Some often have fine, parallel flaking. None of the specimens shown are pristine examples—all apear to be altered or damaged by use, resharpening, and/or, in the case of the one on far right, fire.Photo by Aaron Norment.Photo by Aaron Norment; drawings by Hal Story.
