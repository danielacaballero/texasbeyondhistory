Clovis blade cores from several sites. The small, expended Kincaid specimen (d), lower right, is not easily recognizable as such, and is dwarfed by the more classic examples (a-c) from other sites. At least six blades were struck from the Kincid specimen, according to Collins. Sites from which the specimens were recovered are (left-right), a, 41LL3; b, Gault; c, Comanche Hill; d, Kincaid. Drawing by Pam Headrick (Plate 3.6). From Clovis Blade Technology by Michael Collins (1999, UT Press, Austin).
