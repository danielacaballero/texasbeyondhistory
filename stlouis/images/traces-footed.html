Two sherds of a very unusual dark green-brown footed vessel, with ornate scrolled handle and raised floral design, have not be conclusively attributed to one of the cultures at the site. The diminutive vessel has thin, uniform walls and is composed of a very hard paste, glazed in interior and exterior. According to tentative analysis by Nancy Reese, this may have been a molded vessel of German origin. TARL Collections.
