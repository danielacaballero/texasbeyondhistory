Horn Shelter Number 2 is naturally divided into two sections by a large rock ledge, or balcony, extending into the shelter roughly at its mid point. This view is taken from the north section looking south, with the rock ledge shown at forefront below the overhang. An unknown excavator stands next to the metal sheets and boards that were placed over the south end excavations at the end of each work session as protection from the elements. Photo by Calvin Smith.
