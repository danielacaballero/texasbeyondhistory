The Packsaddle Mountain shines with a golden light at sunset, a spectacle described in the diaries of Spanish expeditions sent in search of the gold and silver promised by native informants. The remains of several mine shafts, along with several early mining tools, has convinced some archeologists and geologists that Packsaddle Mountain is the site of the fabled“ lost silver mine,” Los Almagres.
