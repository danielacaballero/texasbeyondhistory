Credits & Sources

 

J. Philip Dering is the Keeper of the Plant Gallery. Phil is the archeologist for the Texas Forest Service and a consulting archeobotanist and archeologist. He lives in Comstock, Texas, and roams the Lower Pecos Canyonlands in search of fruiting plants.

Glenn T. Goode is the Keeper of the Rock Gallery. Glenn is a consulting archeologist and master flintknapper. He lives near Dripping Springs, Texas, and roams the Edwards Plateau in search of the perfect piece of flint.

Most of the animal photographs were provided by the Texas Parks and Wildlife Department, a partner in TBH.
