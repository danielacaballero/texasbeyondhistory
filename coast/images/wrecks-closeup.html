Enlargement of four-reales silver coin with shield of Charles V of Holy Roman Empire in center. The lions of Leon are shown at upper right and lower left quadrants, and the castles of Castile in upper left and and lower right quadrants. The letter “M” at left of shield denotes the Mexicio City mint; the letter “L” at right is the initial of the assayer. Around the edge of the coin are the words Carolvs et Johanna Reges.
