Partially reconstructed Rockport Incised jar from the Linn Lake area, located near an abandoned channel of the Guadalupe River about 15 miles upstream from San Antonio Bay. This tall jar with a slightly constricted neck and a slightly flaring rim is about 23 centimeters high with a diameter of 12 centimeters.  The white flecks visible in the paste suggest that a minor amount of crushed bone was added as temper.  Vessel reconstructed by Bill Birmingham, on display at the Museum of the Coastal Bend.
