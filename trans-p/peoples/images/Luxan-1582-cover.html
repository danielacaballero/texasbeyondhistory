Cover of the published version of the journal of Diego Pérez de Luxán who chronicled the 1582-1583 Espejo exedition. The group traveled down the Río Conchos, explored the La Junta area, then went up the Rio Grande to New Mexico. The return trip was by way of the Pecos River to the vicinity of Toyah Creek (north-central Reeves County), where they were guided by Jumanos back to La Junta.
