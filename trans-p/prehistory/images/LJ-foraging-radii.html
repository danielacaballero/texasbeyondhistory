Foraging radii away from La Junta, according to the Ydoiaga account of 1747. The inner, shaded circle corresponds to a 20 league (96 km) radius away from modern day Presidio, Texas. The larger circle is a 30 league radius, noted by the Spanish as being where La Juntans would occasionally hunt for bison. Base map by David Hart, CBBS, Sul Ross.
