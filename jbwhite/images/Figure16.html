The major excavations at the J. B. White site focused on Zone 2 in the Main Block, seen here partially completed in the foreground. The Main Block would eventually cover an area of 1,829 square feet (170 square meters). Two smaller blocks were also excavated; they are under the black tarps in this photograph. These East and South Blocks would add another 430 square feet (40 square meters) to the total area excavated.
