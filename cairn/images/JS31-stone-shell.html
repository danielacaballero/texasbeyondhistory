Stone tools and ornament. Top row (left to right: unifacial scraper, triangular biface (both from Cairn 1C); and barbed arrow point found, according to Ray, in an intrusive burial on top of Cairn 3. Bottom row: perforated marine conch shell pendant (described by Ray as "white stone") found near wrist of skeleton in Cairn Number 4, and a polished fine-grained stone artifact (possible boatstone or atlatl weight) found on wrist bones beside it. Inset from Ray 1933: Plate 8.
