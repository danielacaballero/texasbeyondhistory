Variation in arrow points from a single campsite in the Abilene area. The overall shape and slightly constricting stem of the Moran type suggests it may have been the stylistic antecedent to the Perdiz arrow point, emblematic of the later Toyah interval. Moran also resembles Deadman's points of the Palo Duro cultural area. Photo by Laura Nightengale, TARL Sayles Collections (Abilene 2.4).
