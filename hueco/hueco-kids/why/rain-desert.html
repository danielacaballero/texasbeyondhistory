Just how much rain falls in the Chihuahuan Desert?

Usually there’s not much. The average in the Hueco Tanks area is about 8 inches per year. Compare that to an average of nearly 32 inches per year in Austin in central Texas or 42 inches in Houston! Most of the rainfall at Hueco Tanks occurs in summer and early fall. There is also lots of sunshine and low humidity, which adds up to a high rate of evaporation. Water that is on the ground or in the rocks dries up fast, unless it’s protected by a shady rock overhang.
