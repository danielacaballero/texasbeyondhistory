Comparisons of house forms during the Formative period in the western Trans-Pecos. The upper two rows show examples of structures of the Mesilla Phase. The lower row includes examples of isolated rooms from the transitional Dona Ana phase and El Paso phase. At bottom left are House 1 and House 6 from Hueco Tanks. Graphic by Myles Miller from Perttula 2004 (Fig. 7.21).
