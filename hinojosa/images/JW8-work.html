Excavations in progress near the end of the field season. The archeologist on the left (assistant field director Al McGraw) maps the artifacts which have been exposed and left in place. On the right Courtenay Jones (standing) and Cecil Peel excavate a standard unit-level (10 cm thick, 1-x-1 meter square). Also visible are the assorted tools and mapping equipment as well as a trailer used to store tools and supplies on site.
