Black's 1986 Hinojosa site report is hard to miss on library shelves. But like most technical reports, it is rarely seen by non-archeologists, and its 302 pages are filled with scholarly details that most people would consider arcane. In this exhibit you have the opportunity to judge for yourself by consulting one of the many PDF documents scanned from Black's report, courtesy of TBH partner organization, the Center for Archaeological Research at the University of Texas at San Antonio.
