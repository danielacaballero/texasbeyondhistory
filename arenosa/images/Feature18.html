Feature 18, the disarticulated partial remains of a young adult male Bison antiquus, was found in Stratum 40 and dates to sometime before 9000 B.C. It is unclear whether the bones represent an early human presence at Arenosa, but this is hinted at by the presence of two limestone cobbles that seem too large to have been transported to the site by nature. ANRA-NPS Archives at TARL.
