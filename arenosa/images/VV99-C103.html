Close-up of strata 18-12-a series of thin, discrete layers alternating between sterile flood deposits (17, 15, 13, and 12) and cultural deposits (18, 16, and 14). These strata represent a very brief period of time lasting only from about 700 to 600 B.C., and are difficult to distinguish in many parts of the site. ANRA-NPS Archives at TARL.
