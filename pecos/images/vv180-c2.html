This set of small rockshelters (collectively, they were recorded by Graham and Davis as site VV18, a pure rock art site) is located on a high cliff overlooking the lower Devils River. This is one of the most spectacular site settings in the Amistad basin. The site was one of the rock art sites revisited and documented by David Gebhard in 1958. Photo from ANRA-NPS Archives at TARL.
