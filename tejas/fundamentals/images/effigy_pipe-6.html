Effigy pipe of male figure crouching and smoking what appears to be (upon close inspection) to be a frog effigy pipe. Made of red flint clay from near Cahokia. The pipe is 20.5 centimeters high and 36.5 centimeters long. Click to enlarge and see back of pipe. Courtesy Robert Bell and the Sam Noble Oklahoma Museum of Natural History, University of Oklahoma.
