Plan of a Late Caddo community cemetery (about A.D. 1600-1700) at the Taylor Farms site near Lake O' the Pines within the valley of Big Cypress Creek in northeast Texas. The University of Texas excavated this site in 193 and documented 64 graves. Collectors dug up an additional 7 graves. Note the consistent east-west grave orientation and the fact that the graves do not intrude into one another.
