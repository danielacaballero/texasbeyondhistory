Pottery Galleries

These galleries highlight a few examples of the extraordinary Caddo Pottery Tradition. The Ancient Gallery features Caddo pottery vessels from northeast Texas that are in the collections of the Texas Archeological Research Laboratory. The Modern Gallery features pottery made by Jeraldine Redcorn, the Caddo woman who is reviving the lost tradition of her ancestors. All of the photographs were taken by Sharon Mitchell.

In each gallery, you can click on any picture to learn more about each piece, see an image that can be rotated 360 degrees, or open up an enlarged image.
