Hixson Bottle
Height = 6.2 inches (15.5 cm), diameter = 4 inches (10 cm).

This small Caddo-style bottle was made by Chuck Hixson, an archeologist based in Kingsland, Texas, who is also an accomplished potter. He has replicated many Southwestern pottery styles. He engraved this bottle with a flint flake after it was fired as he thinks ancient Caddo potters often did. From the collection of Laura Nightengale.
