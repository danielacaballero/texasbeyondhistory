Rattlesnake Bottle
height = 8 inches (20 cm), diameter = 5 inches (13 cm).

The design is based on a Caddo original excavated in 1967 in Camp County, Texas during the annual field school of the Texas Archeological Society. Kerza Prewitt's 1967 Brownie photograph of the find was blown up and used by Redcorn to recreate the design in 1996. From the collection of Kerza and Elton Prewitt.
