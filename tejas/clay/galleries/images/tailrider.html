Tail Rider Bowl
height = 3.1 inches (8 cm), bowl diameter = 6 inches (15 cm)

This bowl replicates a Fulton Effigy bowl in the "tail rider" style. The archeological original upon which Redcorn based her pot was excavated at the Battle site in 1911 by C. B. Moore. To her, the "head" figure is that of a turkey. Made in 1995, from the collection of Tim Perttula.
