Black Bottle #1
height = 6 inches (15 cm), diameter = 5.5 inches (14 cm).

This engraved spool-neck bottle was made by Jereldine Redcorn in 1995. The design, which was inspired by designs she found on Late Caddo pottery from Arkansas, has become one of her favorites. From the collection of Tim Perttula.
