Distinctive potsherds such as these fragments of Late Caddo pottery from the A.C. Saunders site are useful clues, but they alone are nothing more than fired bits of clay. It is the association of such clues with dated contexts (like a house or a burial) that gives meaning to mere fragments of the past. TARL archives.
