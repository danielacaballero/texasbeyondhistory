Schematic sketch of late Fourche Maline mass burial of 14 individuals neatly laid out in a rectangular pit beneath Mound B at Crenshaw. This 1935 sketch was done by Glen Martin of Texarkana who was employed by Judge Lemley to run the dig. The two intruding pits are later early Caddo shaft tombs that were dug through the mass grave. Courtesy Frank Schambach.
