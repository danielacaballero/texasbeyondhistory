Subject Area

Grade Range

4th, 7th

TEKS

Social Studies, 4th Grade

  • Social Studies 114.6 (1A), identify Native-American groups in Texas and describe the regions in which they lived
  • Social Studies 114.6 (1B), compare the ways of life of Native-American groups in Texas
  • Social Studies 114.6 (22A use primary and secondary sources
  • Social Studies 114.6 (22B),  analyze information by sequencing
  • Social Studies 114.6 (22C), organize and interpret information in timelines

Social Studies, 7th Grade

  • Social Studies 113.23 (1C), explain the significance of the following dates: 1836,1845 and 1861
  • Social Studies 113.23 (4A), Identify events and issues during early Texas statehood
  • Social Studies 113.23 (6A),  identify significant individuals, events, and issues from Reconstruction through the beginning of the 20th century
