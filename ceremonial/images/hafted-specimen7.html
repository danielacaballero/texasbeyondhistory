Hafted Dart Point Analysis: Specimen 7

Specimen 7 (96567) from Chavez Cave, Dona Ana, New Mexico. Type classification of this point is indeterminate; there are some similarities to San Pedro and type Hueco, although serrated edges on Hueco points are uncommon. Peabody Museum, Harvard University. Photo by Laura Nightengale.
