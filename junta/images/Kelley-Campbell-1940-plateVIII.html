Artifacts from the Shiner site (mostly) showing their relative stratigraphic positions. Some of the items were found on the floor of House 2, including the two "cloud blower" stone pipes, suggesting that this house burned while it was still in use.

The two pottery sherds in the upper right (items F and G) are from the Williams site. The large sherd with black and white designs was found in association with a deteriorated human burial. This broken pot could represent a Chihuahua Polychrome vessel from Casas Grandes. From Kelley, Campbell, and Lehmer 1940, Plate VIII.
