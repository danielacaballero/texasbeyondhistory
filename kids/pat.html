Meet an Archeologist

Patricia Mercado-Allinger
State Archeologist for Texas
Texas Historical Commission
Specialty: Texas archeology

Hi:
I first became interested in archeology when I was a young girl, walking around the golf course with my father. Only instead of golf balls, I found arrowheads! I became very interested in how ancient people lived, so I went to school and learned all I could. My job now is to help people protect our archeological heritage. I'll tell you more about Texas archeology in this short video!
http://www.tpwd.state.tx.us/expltx/eft/tas/theater.htm
I'm the one in the red cap on the right working with other archeologists.
