Meet an Archeologist

Tiffany Osburn
Research archeologist
Texas Historical Commission
Specialty: geoarcheology, historical archeology

Hey there!
I've been going to the Texas Archeological Society's field schools every summer since I was 14. Now I'm going to graduate school to learn more about how the land changes over time---and how most ancient habitats get covered up with dirt. It's a long story, but let's just say I like dirt and I like archeology! Recently I worked at the Fort Saint Louis site on the Texas coast. Now that was some bad dirt!
