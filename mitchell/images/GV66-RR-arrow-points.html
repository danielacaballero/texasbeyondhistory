Some 75% of the arrow points from Mitchell Ridge are of the Perdiz type including all of those across the top row. These date to the Final Late Prehistoric period. The second from the right in the bottom row is a complete Cuney point, thought to date to the Protohistoric era. The one on the far right on the bottom row is a Lozenge point made on a blade that may date to the Protohistoric period.
