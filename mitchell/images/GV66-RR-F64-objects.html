Undecorated whooping crane whistles and a shell bracelet made of Style III conch columella beads accompanying 13-15 year-old female, Burial 1, Feature 64, an Early Historic grave with multiple interments. The whistles were near her lower right arm, in her right hand was a cluster of pea-sized pebbles probably representing a rattle. The tubular shell beads encircled her lower right arm.
